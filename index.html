LEARN

GEneSTATION is a novel data portal that aims to synthesize diverse omics and evolutionary genomic data for pregnancy-related tissues and phenotypes, aiming to advance understanding of the genetic underpinnings of preterm birth. GEneSTATION is maintained by investigators at the Ohio Collaborative, one of the five prematurity research centers established with the generous support of the March of Dimes.

DISCOVER

GEneSTATION contains gene expression data from more than 100 genome-wide pregnancy studies across multiple gestational tissues and pathologies. In the first systematic analysis of this data, published recently in the journal BMC Medical Genomics, GEneSTATION scientists synthesize the landscape of PTB transcriptomics research to further our understanding of the genes and pathways involved in PTB subtypes.
